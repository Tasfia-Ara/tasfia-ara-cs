About

Hi I'm Tasfia, an Honours Computer Science and Molecular Biology student at the University of Toronto, currently working as Software Engineering Fellow at Headstarter and an AI Program Mentor at SureStart .

My interests lie in a multitude of software engineering aspects, specifically software design, project management, data analysis, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. To explore these areas in depth, I competed in the 2024 Magna Sustainability Case Competition as well as the 2024 Qualcomm Engineering Case Competition where I served as a Software Designer, earning my team first place nationally in both competitions. Furthermore, I am an active participant in the Women in Science and Engineering (WiSE) club at the University of Toronto, demonstrating my commitment towards equity and inclusion in STEM.
